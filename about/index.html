Our manifesto

The world doesn't work
the way it used to.
Neither do we.

Old organisations were built to optimise — to standardise, scale, and protect what they know. That worked when change was slow and direction was clear. It doesn't work anymore.

Technology is now rewriting itself every few months. AI, immersive media, spatial computing, realtime systems — these aren't tomorrow's problems. They're this morning's. And they reward organisations that can move, sense, and adapt in weeks, not years.

DreamLab is our answer to that. A network, not a hierarchy. A collective, not a roster. Assembled around every brief, tuned to every signal — and designed to stay useful no matter how fast the ground shifts.

The old organisation

Built for stability

  • Fixed departments and siloed expertise
  • Quarterly cycles and annual plans
  • Processes that predate the technology
  • Hierarchy protects the status quo
  • Knowledge is frozen in playbooks
DreamLab

Built for change

  • A 44+ specialist network, assembled per brief
  • Weekly iterations and live delivery
  • Practice that absorbs new tech as it lands
  • Flat collective — the work leads, not titles
  • Always-on scanning of data, signals & trends

History & future

From day one
to day next.

DreamLab launched in June 2024 with a simple conviction — that the North West UK could host a world-class creative technology ecosystem. Less than two years in, the collective has supported over 100 businesses, delivered 35+ projects, and channelled £2.7M of investment and new business directly into the north west.
