About

An R&D lab built for the creative industries

AI & Machine Learning Immersive XR Realtime Technology Creative Tech

DreamLab is a creative technology agency based at labs across MediaCity, Manchester, Liverpool and Cumbria — exploring and developing products, services and experiences at the cutting edge of technology and creativity.

We bring together over 44 deep tech specialists across AI, immersive experiences, virtual production, games, film, music, animation, and more. Working with businesses of all sizes, from SMEs to global brands, we help organisations harness emerging technology to build something genuinely new.
